On September 10, 2022 I launched a program called the Hope. It is a 4-month project in Nyeri that runs every Saturday for 2-3 hours. The goal of this project is to :
Equip children with basic computer skills while incorporating activities in mathematics and science.
Expose the children to various careers within and outside STEM.
Encourage the students to explore the world of technology and STEM fields.
Strive to instill in each child the belief that they can pursue their dreams and succeed regardless of their background.
Promote the development of soft skills such as leadership, teamwork, and problem-solving.

I am currently working with Upendo Children's Home in my community in Nyeri which hosts about 25 children. Some topics we will cover by the end of the program include Word Processing with Microsoft Word, Presentations with PowerPoint, Leadership, Career Exploration, Introduction to Computer Programming, and more!

Over the last 8 Weeks these children have inspired me through their digiligence and curiosity to continue developing my skills so I can achieve my dreams.

This project has been fruitful, and the children have progressed from not knowing how to use a computer to being able to have fun and learn with one. A challenge we have had is the lack of enough computers for the children to learn.
